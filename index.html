About us

Medyan is a Quality of life initiative lead by General Commission for Audiovisual Media (GCAM). The purpose of Medyan is to be a conducive Saudi digital and media ecosystem where talent and business can experiment and grow to become the first choice for media and digital professionals.

Our services

Media Café

Media Café is a luxurious membership-only business club. Members will have access to the most exclusive network in Saudi with all required media services.

Office Space

Medyan will ensure the media companies a dedicated space that accommodates their ever-changing needs, with space options, wide array of sizes and modern fit out.

Creative Hub

Creativity hub where the community of talents and start-ups are located, they will utilize the space and offices to exchange knowledge and training to increase the local production.
